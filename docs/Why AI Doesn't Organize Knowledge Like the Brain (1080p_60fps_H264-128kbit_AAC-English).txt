How can this work in the brain?
It's nothing like a computer.
Modern computers rely on addresses.

This constraint changes everything.

The only way to represent sequences is through linked connectivity.
One element activating the next.
If A comes before B, there must be a path from A to B.

In other words, the brain must use linked structures, not indexed arrays.
Traversal replaces lookup.
Propagation replaces jumping.
The flow of activation itself becomes the mechanism of order.
